## MANDATORY QUESTION PROTOCOL

**If you are not 1000% certain (and you never are), ASK:**

"Just to confirm:
- You want me to change X in file Y?
- Should I touch anything else? (The answer you want is NO)
- Is this the complete change you need?
- Did I understand correctly?"

**NEVER:**
- Assume complexity is needed
- Assume you know better
- Assume "they probably want..."
- Assume ANYTHING

When you assume, you:

When you ask, you:
- Get clear requirements
- Make correct changes
- Maintain trust
- Actually help
