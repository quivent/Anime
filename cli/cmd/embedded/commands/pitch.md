Execute comprehensive elevator pitch generation protocol for project marketing, value proposition creation, and compelling presentation development.

Usage: Generate persuasive elevator pitch markdown files for projects with market analysis, value proposition, and compelling narrative structure.

**Elevator Pitch Generation Framework:**

🎯 **Phase 1: Project Discovery & Core Value Identification**
- Project purpose analysis and mission statement extraction
- Core functionality assessment and unique value proposition
- Target audience identification and market positioning
- Competitive advantage analysis and differentiation factors

📊 **Phase 2: Market Context & Problem Definition**
- Market opportunity analysis and size estimation
- Problem statement articulation and pain point identification
- Current solution landscape and gap analysis
- Customer need validation and urgency assessment

💡 **Phase 3: Solution Positioning & Innovation Highlights**
- Solution architecture and approach explanation
- Innovation points and technical breakthroughs
- Feature highlights and capability demonstration
- Technology stack advantages and implementation benefits

🚀 **Phase 4: Value Proposition & Business Impact**
- Business value quantification and ROI estimation
- Cost savings identification and efficiency gains
- Revenue opportunity analysis and growth potential
- Strategic advantage creation and market positioning

👥 **Phase 5: Audience Targeting & Message Customization**
- Stakeholder analysis and audience segmentation
- Message customization for different stakeholder groups
- Technical vs. business language optimization
- Emotional appeal integration and engagement enhancement

📈 **Phase 6: Traction & Validation Evidence**
- Development progress and milestone achievements
- User feedback and validation evidence
- Performance metrics and success indicators
- Partnership opportunities and market interest

🎨 **Phase 7: Narrative Structure & Compelling Storytelling**
- Hook creation and attention-grabbing opening
- Problem-solution narrative flow optimization
- Call-to-action development and next steps
- Visual elements and presentation enhancement

✨ **Phase 8: Pitch Refinement & Output Generation**
- Message clarity optimization and conciseness improvement
- Multiple format generation and audience adaptation
- Presentation timing and delivery optimization
- Markdown formatting and professional presentation

**Pitch Generation Strategy:**
1. **Discovery Phase** - Analyze project and extract core value
2. **Market Analysis** - Understand context and opportunity
3. **Solution Positioning** - Highlight innovation and capabilities
4. **Value Articulation** - Quantify business impact and benefits
5. **Audience Adaptation** - Customize message for stakeholders
6. **Evidence Integration** - Include validation and traction
7. **Narrative Optimization** - Structure compelling story
8. **Output Generation** - Create polished markdown presentation

**Pitch Templates:**
- **Investor Pitch** - Focus on market opportunity and ROI
- **Technical Pitch** - Emphasize innovation and architecture
- **Executive Pitch** - Highlight strategic value and business impact
- **Customer Pitch** - Focus on problem-solving and benefits
- **Partner Pitch** - Emphasize collaboration and mutual value

**Output Format:**
- 🎯 **Executive Summary** - One-page compelling overview
- 📊 **Market Opportunity** - Problem and solution context
- 💡 **Innovation Highlights** - Technical breakthroughs and advantages
- 🚀 **Value Proposition** - Business impact and benefits
- 👥 **Target Audience** - Customer segments and use cases
- 📈 **Traction Evidence** - Progress and validation metrics
- 🎨 **Call to Action** - Next steps and engagement opportunities

**Success Metrics:**
- ✅ **Clarity Score**: Message comprehensibility and engagement
- ✅ **Value Communication**: Effective benefit articulation
- ✅ **Audience Resonance**: Stakeholder-specific appeal
- ✅ **Narrative Flow**: Story structure and logical progression
- ✅ **Call-to-Action Strength**: Compelling next steps
- ✅ **Professional Presentation**: Polish and visual appeal

**Pitch Configuration Options:**
- `--audience [investor|technical|executive|customer|partner]`: Target audience focus
- `--format [brief|standard|comprehensive]`: Pitch length and detail level
- `--style [formal|conversational|technical]`: Communication tone preference
- `--output [markdown|presentation|email]`: Output format selection
- `--evidence`: Include detailed traction and validation data

Target path: $ARGUMENTS (defaults to current working directory)

The pitch protocol will analyze the project, extract compelling value propositions, and generate professional elevator pitch markdown files optimized for target audiences and maximum impact.
